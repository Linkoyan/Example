# Reel Masters

A relaxing 2D fishing game where you explore lakes, rivers and the open sea to catch over 50 species of fish.

## Features

- **Multiple biomes** — freshwater lakes, mountain rivers, deep ocean
- **50+ fish species** — each with unique behavior and rarity
- **Day/night cycle** — some fish only appear at certain times
- **Upgrade your gear** — rods, reels, bait and boats
- **Leaderboards** — compete for the biggest catch

## How to Play

1. Choose a fishing spot on the map
2. Select your bait and cast your line
3. Wait for a bite and time your reel!
4. Sell your catch to buy better equipment
